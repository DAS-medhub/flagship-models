Our AI Health Models

ChestExpert

Our flagship AI diagnostic system for detecting 14 chest abnormalities from X-ray images with radiologist-level accuracy. Trusted by healthcare professionals worldwide for its precision and reliability in chest disease diagnosis.

ChestExpert Diagnostic System

ChestExpert is our premier AI diagnostic tool that analyzes chest X-rays to detect 14 different abnormalities including pneumonia, tuberculosis, pneumothorax, and cardiac abnormalities. Trained on over 100,000 chest X-rays and validated by expert radiologists, it provides instant, accurate diagnoses to support clinical decision-making in emergency and primary care settings.

As our flagship model, ChestExpert represents the cutting edge of medical AI technology, with continuous updates and improvements based on the latest research and clinical feedback.

Triage Avatar

A trained avatar that triages patients giving them right recommendation on whether to have a consultation with a doctor, get drug from a pharmacy or visit a hospital as well providing the patients the right first aid guidance depending on the severity of their ailment.

Triage Avatar Assistant

The Triage Avatar is an AI-powered virtual assistant that evaluates patient symptoms in real-time, providing personalized recommendations for care. It determines the appropriate level of medical attention—whether a doctor's consultation, pharmacy visit, or hospital emergency—and delivers immediate first aid instructions tailored to the condition's severity.

Designed for accessibility, it supports multiple languages and integrates seamlessly with telehealth platforms to streamline patient triage and improve healthcare efficiency.
